Durian FHC — Stage History Lead Dashboard
Built independently · Praised by manager and client · In active use
Open live dashboard

Every week, answering "how many walk-ins did we get from paid campaigns this month" meant pulling from three separate places: the ad platforms, a Zoho CRM snapshot, and a Stage History export — then joining them manually. I built this to do that automatically.

The core insight was using the full Stage History log instead of a CRM stage snapshot — so every transition a lead ever made is tracked, not just where it sits right now. That's what makes the paid vs. organic walk-in split accurate: a lead that walked in and later moved to "Quoted" doesn't disappear from the walk-in count. The dashboard tracks 18 pipeline stages across 6 store locations and 3 platforms (Google, Meta, Organic), with Cost-per-Walk-in as the primary efficiency metric throughout.

9 views in the dashboard
PAID PERFORMANCE
Google + Meta spend, leads, CPL by month. Platform mix chart and lead journey breakdown.
LOCATION · PAID + ORGANIC
Walk-in rate by store. Paid vs organic split — shows which locations organic out-pulls paid, and vice versa.
WEEK-ON-WEEK TRENDS
Leads, walk-ins, and CPW trending week by week. Google vs Meta vs Total.
DROP REASONS
13 drop-reason categories tracked (Budget Concern, No Response, Wrong Number, etc.), by location and furthest stage reached before Closed Lost.
SPEND & COST PER WALK-IN
CPW by location, Google vs Meta vs Total. All-location and per-location month-on-month view.
PLAN VS ACHIEVE
Monthly budget vs actual, planned CPL vs achieved CPL, directly from the media plan. Per location and overall.
META CREATIVES
CPW by creative Category (Kitchen/Wardrobe), Format (Video/Carousel/Image), Theme, and ad-name. Creative Set drilldown included.
LEAD QUALITY
Quality funnel: % of all leads reaching Interested, Walk-in, or beyond. Cut by location, platform, and Meta creative.
LEAD EXPLORER
Deal-level view with full stage journey. Searchable by deal number, location, owner, and drop reason.

Durian Furniture — Paid Media Dashboard
Built independently · Same system as FHC, scaled up · In active use
Open live dashboard

Same problem as FHC, at a bigger scale: 52 store-level prospecting campaigns across Google Search, PMax, and Meta (plus RMKT), on a combined budget of roughly ₹35L a month. Checking pacing, funnel movement, and cost-efficiency meant pulling each platform separately and a CRM export, then reconciling by hand. This dashboard does that automatically.

It carries over the same core idea from FHC — track the full deal journey, not a point-in-time snapshot — and adds a layer FHC didn't need: cluster and match-type level search structure, plus a Tier 1 / Tier 2 / RMKT split, since Furniture's prospecting is organized around store clusters rather than single locations.

8 view groups in the dashboard
STORE & CHANNEL PACING
Combined budget pacing across all campaign types, by store and by channel. Planned vs actual reach for every prospecting store.
SEARCH STRUCTURE
Cluster performance by campaign, Cluster × Match Type breakdown, Exact vs Phrase split.
TIER & RMKT SPLIT
Tier 1 vs Tier 2 vs RMKT performance, and CRM leads by selected store for remarketing.
SPEND & EFFICIENCY
Spend, leads, and CPL; spend, walk-ins, and CPW; cost per walk-in by store; store visits vs CPSV.
FUNNEL & STAGE TIMING
Deals reaching each stage by monthly cohort, stage timing, and speed to walk-in from lead creation.
TRENDS
Week-on-week and month-on-month performance, daily and weekly spend vs walk-ins vs CPW.
AUDIENCE INSIGHTS
Furniture interest and purchase urgency signals feeding into audience-level insights.
STORE TABLES
Full store table and store rollup, plus a prospecting-only store table for cluster-level review.
